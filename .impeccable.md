## Design Context

### Users
* **Target Audience**: Aspiring models, established and emerging fashion designers, professional make-up artists, high-fashion photographers, event production staff, and luxury fashion enthusiasts/sponsors.
* **Context**: Viewing on mobile or desktop to apply for the upcoming Doral Fashion Week edition or to explore past editions.
* **Job to be Done**: Review past event seasons, understand application criteria, and fill out a digitally signed legal agreement to apply for roles.

### Brand Personality
* **Voice**: Exclusive, elite, and visually commanding.
* **Tone**: Luxury/Refined, editorial, and sophisticated.
* **3-Word Personality**: Elite, Avant-garde, Golden.

### Aesthetic Direction
* **Visual Tone**: Immersive Dark Mode with fine typography and warm gold accents (`#D4AF37`). High-end editorial editorial layouts reminiscent of Vogue or premium design agencies.
* **Theme**: Deep off-black (`#050505`) with a subtle analog noise/grain overlay.

### Design Principles
1. **Asymmetric Grid Balance**: Avoid repetitive grid patterns. Use a bento-like grid for application roles, highlighting key divisions (Models and Designers) with more visual weight.
2. **Editorial Typography**: Capitalize on the contrast between the serif font `Quotesa` (luxurious, high contrast) and the geometric `Visby CF` body text.
3. **Subtle Motion & Depth**: Ensure micro-interactions (hover, focus, transitions) are ultra-smooth (using ease-out-expo curves). Use high-performance GPU animations (transform and opacity only).
4. **No Placeholders / Full Polish**: Avoid empty links, repetitive placeholder texts, or raw borders. Add polished copywriting, high-contrast imagery, and detailed inputs.
